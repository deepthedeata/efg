About Us

EFG (EduFinFlex Growth) is an innovative school fee financing company headquartered in Bangalore, India. Our mission is to make education affordable and stress-free by providing flexible EMI options for school and professional course fees.

We believe no student should be held back due to financial constraints. Our transparent, tech-enabled platform helps parents and students pay fees conveniently while focusing on education.
